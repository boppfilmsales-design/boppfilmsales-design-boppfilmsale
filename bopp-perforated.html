Bopp Perforated Film

BOPP/CPP macro and micro perforated film. Material items: BOPP/CPP micro perforated film & BOPP/CPP macro perforated film.

Micro Perforated Film · 微孔打孔膜
Normally, micro perforated's density is: P160, this is: 160 holes per square inch. TD (Machine Direction): 2mm, MD (Transverse Direction): 2 mm.
Macro Perforated Film · 宏观打孔膜
Macro perforated, also called air hole, usually, diameter of the hole is 1cm, space between holes (hole pitch) is: 3"(76mm). All the hole diameter would be approx 8mm, distance between whole holes is 4cm.
