BOPP Thermal Lamination Film Glossy

BOPP热复合薄膜（光亮） · BOPP + EVA热复合薄膜（光亮面）
Bopp Thermal lamination Films

Environmentally friendly: It is environmentally friendly, nonhazardous to health and safe to use. No toxic gases or volatile content is emitted. Safety in laminating process and no solvent based adhesive is used.

High function: Compared with other solvent lamination, our film is better in clarity and bonding. Strong ability of absorbing power. It prevents lamination from being pressed, bubbled and shredded off easily. Matt thermal lamination film is good for spot UV and hot stamping.

Easy handling: It is easy to operate once the required temperature is met and no special technique required.

Efficient and energy-saving: Production costs is reduced substantially and there is no film wastage, mixture of adhesive solvent, and no UV heating lamp is required.

Adaptable to different kinds of machines: This film is recommended to be used in dry laminator. Small adjustments need to be made to the machine if the film is used in wet laminator.
BOPP热复合薄膜

环保：环保，对健康无害，使用安全。不排放有毒气体或挥发性物质。复合过程安全，不使用溶剂基粘合剂。

高性能：与其他溶剂复合相比，我们的薄膜在透明度和粘合方面更好。强吸收能力。防止复合件被压皱、起泡和轻易撕裂。哑光热复合薄膜适用于局部UV和烫金。

易操作：一旦达到所需温度，操作简单，无需特殊技术。

高效节能：生产成本大幅降低，无薄膜浪费、粘合剂溶剂混合，无需UV加热灯。

适用于各类机器：该薄膜建议在干式复合机中使用。如果在湿式复合机中使用，需要对机器进行小幅调整。
