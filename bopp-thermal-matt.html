BOPP Thermal Lamination Film matt, BOPP MATT film coated with EVA.

Spec.:
1.) Jumbo roll sizes:
Total Thickness: 22-33UM (Mic.)
Film thickness: 10, 12, 15, 18, 20 UM (Mic.)
Width: 325-1200MM
Length: 3000M (2000M, 4000M, 6000M, 9000M).
Inner core: 76MM, 152MM

2.) Mini-roll sizes:
Total Thickness: 22-33UM (Mic.)
Film thickness: 10, 12, 15, 18, 20 UM (Mic.)
Width: 325-1200MM
Length: 100M, 120M, 150M, 200M
Inner core: 1.5", 1.8", 2.0", 3" (Inches)

Applications: Laminate the surface of Books, catalogs, samples, posters, printed paper, Food, medicines, cosmetics, gifts, wine boxes and other packaging film, Drawings, documents, ads, pictures, display boards, etc. BOPP matt film
23mic=15mic (bopp film) + 8mic (EVA glue)
27mic=15mic (bopp film) + 12mic (EVA glue)
30mic=18mic (bopp film) + 12mic (EVA glue)
Corona treatment side: A (inside); B (outside); A/B (double sides);
Surface tension (corona treated): ≥38dyne

Feature
l Environmental-friendly: Through SGS test and received the certification. No pollution, nontoxic, tasteless and non-benzene.
l Energy-saving: Enhance the efficiency and reduce the cost.
l Safety: No polluted air and materials while laminating. Hidden danger and inflammable dissolvent can be entirely eliminated from the traditional craft.
l Easy to control: Once the temperature, speed and pressure are met the requirement and no need to coat glue, heat, dry and professional technique.
l Excellent Function: Can greatly improve saturation of colors and glabrousness of surface. Strong stickiness, waterproof, moisture-resistant. Matt thermal lamination film is good for spoting UV and hot stamping.

特点
• 环保：通过SGS测试并获得认证。无污染、无毒、无味、无苯。
• 节能：提高效率，降低成本。
• 安全：复合过程中无污染的空气和材料。完全消除传统工艺中的隐患和易燃溶剂。
