Copy Paper Jumbo Rolls

复印纸大卷
High-quality copy paper in jumbo roll format for high-volume printing and copying. Four production lines (5#, 6#, 7#, 8# machines) with monthly output of 8,000 tons. 8# machine is the best production line, stably manufacturing copy paper products for large dealers with yearly long-term contracts. In China, we are one of the fewer factories who can offer only copy paper products per month up to this quantity. We import raw material (wooden pulp) from overseas like Russia and other areas. We are also one of the fewer factories who can offer only copy paper products in quality actual 100% wooden pulp best in China. Brands: Phoenix, Orient, China Tiger. Available in 70/75/80 GSM with brightness from 94-96% (mixed pulp) to 104-106% (100% wooden pulp). Customer logo available on ream wrappers and carton wrappers for huge/medium-order and steady-order customers.
