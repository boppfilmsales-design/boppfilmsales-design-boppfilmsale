Bopp Pearlized Film (White Opaque)

BOPP珠光白色不透明薄膜
BOPP Pearlized Film (also known as BOPP White Opaque Film) is a white opaque co-extruded BOPP film with pearlescent luster. Heat sealable on both sides with excellent opacity and printability. Ideal for premium packaging applications.
BOPP珠光薄膜（又称BOPP白色不透明薄膜）是一种白色不透明共挤BOPP薄膜，具有珠光光泽。双面可热封，具有优异的不透明度和印刷性能。适用于高端包装应用。
