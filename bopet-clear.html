Details & Specifications

Bopet Film

BOPET film (biaxially oriented Polyester films) featuring good thermal stability and tenacity; low air and water permeability; applicable to vacuum aluminum plating, compound package, cooking bag manufacture, etc.

Featuring:
Having the good adhesion with ink and coating, can be used for multi-color printings with good flatness. Is mainly used in heat-sealing bags of frozen food, industrial products and cosmetics packaging.

Specification:
thickness: 12 microns-25 microns, 75 microns-150 microns, 185 microns-380 microns, 500 microns
width: 200-1200MM
length: 500m, 1000m, 1500m, 4000m, 6000m, 9000m, 12000m

Polyester film is wound around paper shaft with an internal diameter of 76.2mm-152.4mm and covered with polyethylene film; each coil is supported by damper and put in carton; medium and large package needs wooden mount.

Customer's own requirement is available for us such as printed OEM logos and private label or particular size and package style.
BOPET薄膜

BOPET薄膜（双向拉伸聚酯薄膜）具有良好的热稳定性和韧性；低透气性和透湿性；适用于真空镀铝、复合包装、蒸煮袋制造等。

特性：
与油墨和涂层具有良好的附着力，可用于多色印刷，平整度好。主要用于冷冻食品热封袋、工业产品包装和化妆品包装。

规格：
厚度：12微米-25微米、75微米-150微米、185微米-380微米、500微米
宽度：200-1200毫米
长度：500米、1000米、1500米、4000米、6000米、9000米、12000米

聚酯薄膜缠绕在纸芯上，内径76.2毫米-152.4毫米，外覆聚乙烯膜；每个卷材用减震器支撑并放入纸箱；中型和大型包装需要木托盘。

客户可以定制印刷OEM标志、私有标签或特定尺寸和包装样式。
