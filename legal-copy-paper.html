Legal Size Copy Paper

法律文件尺寸复印纸
Legal size copy paper for legal documents, contracts, and official paperwork. We are one of the fewer factories who can offer copy paper jumbo roll products with GRM from 48 to 100GRM with outstanding quality. Import raw material (wooden pulp) from overseas like Russia and other areas. Also one of the fewer factories who can offer LWC paper products in quality actual 100% wooden pulp. Two types available: Economy type (CIE whiteness 146±3, brightness 103.5±1.5%) and Allround type (CIE whiteness 160±3, brightness 110±1.5%). Core: 76MM (3") or 152.4mm (6"). Max width: 1500mm. Max weight: 2000Kgs.
