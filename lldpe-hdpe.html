Lldpe & Hdpe Film

LLDPE及HDPE薄膜
PE stretch film for pallet packaging. Blown PE stretch film with excellent anti-piercing and stretching ability. Lightweight, strong tenacity, excellent puncture resistance, good self-adhesion. Available in hand-warp and machine-warp grades. Also includes high molecular weight HDPE and LLDPE copolymer films with transparency, tensile strength, anti-fog performance, ideal for food packaging materials. HDPE provides excellent toughness and chemical resistance while LLDPE offers superior flexibility and stretch performance.
PE拉伸膜，用于托盘包装。吹塑PE拉伸膜，具有优异的抗穿刺和拉伸能力，重量轻、韧性强、抗穿刺性好、自粘性强。提供手工包装和机器包装等级。还包括高分子量HDPE和LLDPE共聚薄膜，透明度高、拉伸强度好、防雾性能优异，是食品包装材料的理想选择。HDPE提供优异的韧性和耐化学性，LLDPE则提供卓越的柔韧性和拉伸性能。
