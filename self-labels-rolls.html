🔖 Self-Adhesive Labels Rolls · 自粘标签卷

纸/BOPP/PET自粘标签卷，条码、产品、物流标签
Self-adhesive label materials are paper, film, or other special material for the fabric, coated with adhesive on the back to protect the paper as a substrate coated with silicone paper. After printing, die-cutting and other processing into finished labels. Face material: Film (BOPP/PET/PE), Synthetic Paper, Artpaper. Release liner: PET, Glassine Paper. Printing method: Flexographic / Gravure / Screen / Digital. Colors: Up to 10 colors supported, CMYK + 6 Spot Colors. Applications: Food & Beverage, Cosmetic, Personal Care, Chemical, Automobile.
自粘标签材料是以纸、薄膜或其他特殊材料为基材，背面涂有粘合剂，底纸为硅涂层的保护材料。经印刷、模切等加工后制成成品标签。面材：薄膜（BOPP/PET/PE）、合成纸、艺术纸。离型纸：PET、格拉辛纸。印刷方式：柔版/凹版/丝网/数字印刷。颜色：最多支持10色，CMYK+6专色。应用：食品饮料、化妆品、个人护理、化工、汽车。
