4.5 Microns BOPET Film silver metallized

4.5 Microns BOPET Film silver metallized, Bopet polyester metallized film has a super reflective, mirror-like surface. It is a oriented polyester film with a very thin coating of aluminum that is vacuum deposited on to the surface of the film. This film is used in a variety of industrial and applications, including printing, packaging, crafts and many other applications.

Main features of bopet polyester metallized film
a. Good Metal Bond Strength
b. Good Barrier properties of oxygen, light, water vapor
c. Good Lamination Bond Strength
d. Excellent Machinability

4.5微米BOPET薄膜银色镀铝

4.5微米BOPET薄膜银色镀铝，BOPET聚酯镀铝薄膜具有超反射镜面表面。它是一种定向聚酯薄膜，表面真空沉积了极薄的铝涂层。该薄膜用于各种工业和应用，包括印刷、包装、工艺品和许多其他应用。
