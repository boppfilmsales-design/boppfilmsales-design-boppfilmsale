Bops Shrinkage Film

BOPS收缩薄膜
BOPS Shrinkage Film is a biaxially oriented polystyrene film with high shrink properties, designed for shrink sleeve and tamper-evident band applications. It provides excellent clarity, high shrink ratio, and good dimensional stability during the shrinking process.
BOPS收缩薄膜是一种具有高收缩性能的双向拉伸聚苯乙烯薄膜，专为收缩套和防伪带应用设计。具有优异的透明度、高收缩率和良好的尺寸稳定性。
