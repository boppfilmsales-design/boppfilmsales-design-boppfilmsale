Bops Sheets for Food Container

BOPS片材·食品容器
BOPS polystyrene sheets in roll form for food container manufacturing. Available in clear (transparent) and colors (red, green, blue, black, yellow). Used with PET containers, PP containers for food storage crispers. Low dust, balanced gloss and haze, good dimensional stability, consistent gauge tolerance. Widely exported to Middle Asia, South Asia, Middle East, West Africa, Middle America, South America, Russia and beyond. Annual supply capacity of 750,000 tons.
BOPS聚苯乙烯片材（卷状），用于食品容器制造。提供透明及彩色（红、绿、蓝、黑、黄等）选项。与PET容器、PP容器配套使用，制作食品储物盒。低粉尘，光泽与雾度平衡，尺寸稳定性好，厚度公差一致。远销中东亚、南亚、中东、西非、中美洲、南美洲、俄罗斯等地区。年供应量75万吨。
