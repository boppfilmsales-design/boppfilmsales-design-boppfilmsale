Bops Shrinkage Film

BOPS热缩薄膜
BOPS biaxially oriented polystyrene shrink film — a rapidly developed eco-friendly skin-packaging material compliant with environmental requirements. Excellent strength, rigidity, shape stability, gloss and transparency. Convenient processing, easy coloring, high printability with very low attrition rate and high printing resolution. High shrinkage ratio and strength allow tight bonding with containers of various shapes, enabling fine patterns and novel packaging container designs. Non-toxic, tasteless, grease-resistant, meeting food hygiene standards for beverage and commodity labels.
BOPS双向拉伸聚苯乙烯热缩薄膜——一种快速发展的新型环保贴体包装材料，符合环保要求。高强度、高刚性、形状稳定性好，光泽度和透明度优异。加工方便，易着色，印刷性能优异，损耗率低，印刷分辨率高。高收缩率和高强度使其可与各种形状的容器紧密贴合，可印刷精美图案，满足各种新颖包装容器设计需求。无毒无味，耐油脂，符合食品卫生标准。
