05Competitive Pricing

极具竞争力的价格

💰 Factory-Direct Advantage

By integrating manufacturing and export under one group, we eliminate unnecessary middlemen and pass the savings directly to our customers. Our pricing reflects true factory costs without the markups of trading companies or agents.

When you buy from AEC GROUP, you buy directly from the manufacturer. There are no intermediary margins, no agent commissions, and no hidden costs. This integrated structure is the foundation of our genuinely competitive global pricing.

🏭 Regional Cost Advantages

Our manufacturing base in Hefei, Anhui Province benefits from significant regional cost advantages:

  • Labor Costs: Anhui offers skilled manufacturing labor at competitive rates compared to coastal regions
  • Energy Costs: Lower electricity and utility costs reduce overall production expenses
  • Raw Material Access: Proximity to raw material suppliers reduces procurement and logistics costs
  • Industrial Infrastructure: Well-developed industrial parks with favorable operating conditions
  • Transportation: Central China location with access to major ports via the Yangtze River and high-speed rail

📊 Volume Pricing Structure

We offer tiered pricing that rewards larger orders and long-term partnerships:

  • Sample Orders: Small quantities for product evaluation and testing
  • Container Loads: FCL (Full Container Load) pricing for standard orders
  • Bulk Orders: Multi-container and annual contract pricing with significant discounts
  • Long-Term Contracts: Preferential pricing for customers with ongoing, predictable demand
  • Mixed Product Orders: Consolidated pricing for orders across multiple product categories

🔍 Transparent Pricing Policy

We believe in transparent, straightforward pricing with no hidden fees:

  • All prices include standard export packaging
  • Clear quotation with itemized product specifications
  • No surprise charges — what you see is what you pay
  • Flexible Incoterms (FOB, CIF, EXW, DDP) to suit your logistics needs
  • Price validity period clearly stated on every quotation
