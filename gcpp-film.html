Gcpp Film

GCPP镀铝流延聚丙烯薄膜
GCPP cast polypropylene film (general CPP), produced by non-oriented casting process. Good transparency, high gloss, good stiffness, moisture resistance, heat resistance and excellent heat-sealing properties. After printing: suitable for bags, garments, knitwear, flower bags, files, albums, food packaging, barrier packaging and metal film decoration. After vacuum aluminum plating: laminated with BOPP, BOPA for composite packaging of tea, crispy fried foods, biscuits. Heat-resistant with softening point ~140°C for hot-filling, cooking bags, aseptic packaging. Acid and alkali resistant, excellent grease resistance.
GCPP通用流延聚丙烯薄膜，非定向流延工艺生产。透明度高，光泽好，刚性佳，耐湿耐热，热封性能优异。印刷后适用于：服装袋、针织袋、花卉袋、文件夹、相册、食品包装。真空镀铝后与BOPP、BOPA等基材复合，用于茶叶、油炸食品、饼干等高档包装。耐温性好（PP软化点约140°C），可用于热填充、蒸煮袋、无菌包装。耐酸碱，耐油脂性能优异。
