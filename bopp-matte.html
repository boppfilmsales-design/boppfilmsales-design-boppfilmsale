Bopp Matte Film 10,12,15,18 Mic

BOPP哑光薄膜 · 哑光复合双向拉伸聚丙烯薄膜
Specialty BOPP lamination film with fine matte surface texture that produces a soft, premium, low-glare finish. Available in 10, 12, 15 and 18 micron thicknesses. Popular in high-end retail packaging and publishing where a sophisticated, fingerprint-resistant surface is desired.
