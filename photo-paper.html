Photo Paper

相纸
High-quality photo paper with excellent color reproduction and sharp image detail. Available in glossy, matte, and satin finishes for professional and consumer photo printing. Compatible with all major inkjet and dye-sublimation printers. Excellent ink absorption, instant dry, and archival quality options available for long-lasting photo preservation.
