Plain BOPP Cigarette Film with heat sealable matches slow/plain-speed packaging machines.

BOPP cigarette tobacco film generally used for soft/hard tobacco pack and used for modern high/slow packing machines.

transparent biaxially oriented polypropylene cigarette film featured coextruded heat sealable on both sides, incorporated with anti-static protection

Thickness: 18, 19, 20, 21, 22, 25 Microns
Width: 90, 110, 118, 120, 135, 150, 220, 350MM
Length: 2000M, 2800M, 3000M, 3500M

Well-fitting to machine, suitable for high speed packing of all kinds of soft pack. Special hot slip ability on surface, can avoid machine-blocking, sticking etc. Quick heat sealing at low temperature and pressure. Good foldability after forming. Inferior optical properties to others.
