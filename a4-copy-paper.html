A4 Copy Paper

A4复印纸
Standard A4 size copy paper for everyday printing and copying. Grammage: 70/75/80GSM. Sizes available: 8.5"×11", A4, 8.5"×13", A3, 11"×17", 22"×34" and foolscap (17×13.5 inches). Brightness: 95-96%, 98-102%, 106-108%. 100% actual wooden pulp (woodfree). Package: 500 sheets/ream, 5 reams/carton, 7800-8250 reams/20GP without pallet. Delivery within 10 days. No jam in photocopy machine, no double feed, stay flat after copying, leave no dust in the copy-machine. Nice appearance white and clean, nice touch smooth and bulky, no see through for double-sided printing. Developed for photocopy machines, laser printers, ink-jet printers, fax machines.
