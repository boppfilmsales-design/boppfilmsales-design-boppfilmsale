丰富产品系列

🎞️ Flexible Packaging Films

From 3μm capacitor film to 75μm flower wrap, our film portfolio covers the entire spectrum of flexible packaging needs. We produce and export 9 major product categories, all available in custom widths, thicknesses, and specifications.

  • BOPP Film: 10+ types including plain, heat sealable, pearlized, glossy, matte, anti-fog, anti-static, perforated, cigarette, and capacitor grades
  • BOPET Film: Clear, metallized, capacitor, solar backsheet, transfer, and thermal lamination grades
  • CPP Film: General purpose, metallized (MCPP), retort (RCPP), and functional specialty grades
  • BOPS Film: Window envelope film (matt/glossy), shrinkage film, and food container sheets
  • BOPA Film: High-performance nylon film for vacuum, retort, and boil-in-bag applications
  • POF Shrink Film: Center-folded, tubular, and macro perforated cling film
  • Coating Film: PVDC (K-film), acrylic, and PE coating on BOPP/BOPET substrates
  • Packing Tape: BOPP tape jumbo rolls, crystal tape, sealing tape, acrylic tape, printed tape, masking tape
  • Tear Tape: BOPP and polyester tear tape, holographic and plain variants

📄 Paper & Office Supplies

Our paper products division serves both consumer and industrial markets with a comprehensive range of office and packaging papers.

  • Copy Paper: A4, letter, legal, and foolscap sizes in 70/75/80 GSM, brightness grades A/A+/A++
  • Jumbo Rolls: 80 GSM copy paper in jumbo format for high-volume users, 400 reams per ton
  • LWC Paper: Light weight coated paper, 48-100 g/m², for magazines, catalogs, and commercial printing
  • Photo Paper: Glossy, matte, and satin finishes, 180-300 g/m²
  • Inkjet Supplies: Flex banner, photo paper, ink cartridges, and specialty printing media
  • Thermal Paper: POS rolls, fax paper, medical imaging paper
  • Release Liner: Glassine, PE-coated, and kraft silicone release papers

🔧 Industrial Materials & Equipment

Beyond films and papers, we supply a range of industrial materials and equipment for manufacturing and packaging operations.

  • Adhesive Products: BOPP packing tape, double-sided tape, masking tape, adhesive labels
  • Solar & PV: Photovoltaic solar panels, BOPET solar backsheet film
  • Equipment: Slitting machines, rewinding equipment, barcode printers, thermal transfer ribbons
  • Specialty: Wheel rims, protective masks, wax/resin thermal transfer ribbons
