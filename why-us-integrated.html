🏭 Manufacturing Excellence

AEC GROUP combines Anhui East New Materials Co., Ltd. (manufacturing) with Anhui Eastern Communication Import & Export Co., Ltd. (export) — a synergistic combination of manufacturing excellence and international trade expertise.

This integrated structure enables direct factory pricing, quality at source, and a single point of contact for all orders. Our production, R&D, quality control, and export operations work under one group umbrella, ensuring consistency and reliability.

  • Manufacturing Base: 4 modern production plants with total area of 300 acres
  • Annual Output: Over 8,000 tons of copy paper alone, plus thousands of tons of flexible packaging films
  • Workforce: 1,200 employees including 68 high-tech technician talents
  • Certifications: ISO9001 quality management and ISO14001 environmental management
  • R&D Partnerships: University of Science and Technology of China, Anhui University, Hefei Industrial University

🌐 Global Export Focus

With decades of experience in international logistics and customs compliance, we serve clients across food, pharmaceutical, tobacco, printing, electronics, and industrial sectors worldwide. Our integrated approach eliminates unnecessary middlemen, providing genuinely competitive global pricing.

We export to over 60 countries and regions including Middle Asia, South Asia, Middle East, West Africa, Middle America, South America, Russia, and beyond. Our deep experience in LC documentation, export certificates, and regulatory compliance ensures smooth transactions in every major global market.

📦 One-Stop Service

From product development and manufacturing to quality control and international shipping, AEC GROUP provides a complete, seamless service. Our customers benefit from:

  • Direct Factory Pricing: No middlemen, genuine factory-direct costs
  • Quality at Source: Full quality control from raw material to finished product
  • Single Point of Contact: One team handles everything from inquiry to delivery
  • Integrated Logistics: In-house export team manages shipping, customs, and documentation
