Product Description · 产品介绍

Details & Specifications

Precision-surface polyester carrier film engineered for hot stamping foil, transfer metallization and release coating applications. The film's tightly controlled surface quality and dimensional stability ensure consistent, defect-free transfer of metallic or pigmented layers onto the final substrate during the hot stamping or cold foil process.
精密表面聚酯载体薄膜，专为烫金箔、转移镀铝及离型涂层应用而设计。薄膜表面质量及尺寸稳定性经严格控制，确保在烫金或冷烫工艺中将金属层或色料层稳定、无缺陷地转移至最终基材上。
