Vmcpp Film

VMCPP真空镀铝流延膜
VMCPP vacuum-metallized cast polypropylene film for food and medicine flexible packaging, dry-laminated with BOPET, BOPP, BOPA etc. Silver color with excellent aluminum adhesion, bright reflective surface, high barrier properties. Superior moisture and oxygen barrier performance for extended product shelf life. Vacuum metallization ensures uniform aluminum coating with strong adhesion, providing both decorative and functional barrier properties for demanding packaging applications.
VMCPP真空镀铝流延聚丙烯薄膜，用于食品和医药软包装，可与BOPET、BOPP、BOPA等干法复合。银色，铝层附着力强，反射光泽明亮，阻隔性能优异。出色的防潮和氧气阻隔性能，有效延长产品保质期。真空镀铝确保铝层均匀、附着力强，为高要求包装应用提供兼具装饰性和功能性的阻隔性能。
