🚬 Specialized Tobacco Films

High-specification BOPP films engineered for the demanding requirements of cigarette overwrap and tobacco packaging. Manufactured to extremely tight tolerances for high-speed tobacco packaging lines running at thousands of packs per minute.

📦 Cigarette Overwrap

BOPP films specifically designed for cigarette pack overwrap. Extremely low haze, superior low-temperature heat sealability, and high stiffness ensure crisp folds and reliable sealing at production speeds exceeding several hundred packs per minute.

  • Low Haze: Crystal-clear appearance for premium brand presentation
  • Low-Temp Heat Seal: Reliable sealing at 100-140°C
  • High Stiffness: Crisp fold lines and stable pack geometry
  • Low Static: Prevents film jacking on high-speed lines
  • Custom Printing: Brand-specific colors and designs available

🎬 Shrink Wrap & Bundling

POF and BOPP shrink films for multi-pack bundling, carton overwrap, and promotional packaging. High shrink ratio and strong seals ensure secure bundling for transport and retail display.

  • POF Shrink Film: High clarity, high shrink ratio for bundling
  • BOPP Shrink Film: Cigarette multipack overwrap
  • Tear Tape: Easy-open feature on cigarette multipacks
