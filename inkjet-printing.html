Inkjet Printing Supply

喷墨打印耗材
Complete range of inkjet printing supplies including Flex Banner & Photo Paper. Flex Banner is a light box sign consisting of two layers of PVC and high-strength mesh fabric (Bao Li Bu). Production methods: Daogua coating, calendering, paste legitimate. Key quality points: thickness, tensile strength, ductility, weather resistance, light transmittance, flame resistance, peeling, flatness, ink absorption, color reproduction. Three processes: Daogua law, rolling method, and stickers legitimate. Used for wedding photo studios, home decoration, architectural decoration, exhibition halls, museums, stadiums, advertising projects, municipal engineering, hotels, restaurants, retail stores. Slim light box thickness generally less than 3CM, 1/10 of ordinary light box thickness. Maximizes space utilization, beautifies environment, saves transportation costs, simplifies installation.
完整的喷墨打印耗材系列，包括灯箱布和相纸。灯箱布由两层PVC和高强度网格织物（宝丽布）组成。生产方法：刮刀涂层、压延、贴合。质量要点：厚度、拉伸强度、延展性、耐候性、透光率、阻燃性、剥离性、平整度、吸墨性、色彩还原。三种工艺：刮刀法、压延法、贴纸法。用于婚纱摄影、家居装饰、建筑装饰、展览馆、博物馆、体育场、广告项目、市政工程、酒店、餐厅、零售店。超薄灯箱厚度一般小于3厘米，为普通灯箱厚度的1/10。最大化空间利用，美化环境，节省运输成本，简化安装。
