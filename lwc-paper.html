Lwc Paper

轻涂纸 (LWC)
LWC (Light Weight Coated) paper with GRM from 48 to 100g/m² with actual 100% wooden pulp outstanding quality. Import raw material from Russia, Chile and other areas. Front and back coated with thin coating (calcium carbonate and cationic starch), through super calendar to improve physical properties and printability. Chemical composition: 40% long fiber bleached softwood chemical pulp, 60% mechanical wood pulp. Between coated paper and offset paper performance, color printing effect comparable to coated paper, good opacity, smoothness, gloss and printability. Low cost, high value-added. Used for magazines, catalogs, advertising, trademarks, newspaper inserts.
LWC轻涂纸，克重48-100克/平方米，100%纯木浆，质量优异。从俄罗斯、智利等地区进口原料。正面和背面涂有薄涂层（碳酸钙和阳离子淀粉），经超级压光改善物理性能和印刷性能。化学组成：40%长纤维漂白软木化学浆，60%机械木浆。性能介于涂布纸和胶版纸之间，彩色印刷效果媲美涂布纸，不透明度、平滑度、光泽和印刷性能好。成本低，附加值高。用于杂志、产品目录、广告、商标、报纸插页。
