Bops Window Envelope Film Glossy

BOPS窗口信封薄膜·光亮
BOPS biaxially oriented polystyrene film with glossy finish for envelope window applications. High clarity and shine with excellent anti-static properties. Good film smoothness prevents jacking during envelope production. Excellent slitting with high transverse tear strength for high-speed envelope lines. Cost-efficient at standard 25um thickness, saving 8%~16% versus competitor 27um/29um grades. Suitable for envelope manufacturers, bulk mail, postal operators and mail sorting businesses.
BOPS双向拉伸聚苯乙烯光亮薄膜，用于信封窗口。高透明度和光泽度，优异的抗静电性能。薄膜表面光滑，信封生产过程中不产生卡顿。分切性能好，横向撕裂强度高，适合高速信封生产线。标准25um厚度，比竞品27um/29um节省8%~16%成本。
