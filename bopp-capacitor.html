BOPP Capacitor Film (clear film)
With one side corona treatment and the reverse side clear smooth. It homogenizes in thickness, low dielectric loss, high electric strength, excellent processing. Single side corona treated for the Zn or Zn/Al metallized. Mainly used for dielectric materials for low-voltage and parallel power capacitors, AC motor capacitors and DC capacitors.
