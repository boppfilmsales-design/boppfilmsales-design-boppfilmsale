🖨️ Printing & Lamination Films

High-quality BOPP films designed for wet and cold lamination of printed paper substrates. The smooth, high-gloss surface produces a brilliant mirror-like finish that enhances print color vibrancy while protecting against moisture, scuffing, and UV fading.

📖 Book & Magazine Covers

BOPP glossy and matte films optimized for book covers, magazine covers, and catalogue production. Available in standard thicknesses from 8-15 microns for lamination.

  • BOPP Glossy Film: High-gloss finish for vibrant covers
  • BOPP Matte Film: Premium non-glare finish for elegant publications
  • Thermal Lamination: EVA-activated layer for direct thermal bonding

📄 Commercial Printing

Films for posters, brochures, labels, and commercial printing applications. Excellent printability with corona treatment for ink adhesion.

  • High Clarity: Crystal-clear base for vibrant printed graphics
  • Excellent Printability: Corona-treated surface for ink adhesion
  • UV Resistance: Protects printed colors from fading
  • Custom Sizes: Available in widths up to 10,000mm
