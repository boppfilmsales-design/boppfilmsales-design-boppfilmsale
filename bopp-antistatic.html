Bopp Anti Static Film

BOPP抗静电薄膜 · 电子元件包装用抗静电薄膜
BOPP anti static film is used for packing textile relative goods, and it won't stick up dust and atomy, and if your paste this sheets film on the wall, it won't stay on the wall, it will slip from the wall. BOPP/BOPET anti static film also called Polypropylene anti static film, BOPP clothes packaging film, BOPP bag usage film.
