🌸 Flower Wrapping Films

Crystal-clear, moisture-proof BOPP film designed specifically for wrapping fresh flower bouquets and gift items. Combines superior mechanical toughness with excellent transparency to showcase the product while protecting against moisture loss and physical damage during transport.

  • Crystal Clear: High clarity to showcase flower beauty
  • Moisture Barrier: Prevents wilting during transport
  • Mechanical Strength: Tear resistance for secure wrapping
  • Dual Format: Available in continuous rolls and pre-cut sheets

  • BOPP Color Printed: Custom printed flower wrapping with brand logos
  • Flower Sleeve Bags: Pre-made bags for quick flower packaging
