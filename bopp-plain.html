Bopp Plain Film

BOPP普通薄膜 · 印刷级双向拉伸聚丙烯薄膜
Plain BOPP Film (Bi-axially Oriented PolyPropylene) For Printing And Lamination

BOPP film is widely used for plain, lamination, matte and metalized films which are used for many different sorts selection of applications, such as carton box, flower, gift, food, confectionery, lamination, CD, DVD, VCD, cigarette, and box packaging, packing tape, etc according to its purpose such as printing, clothes, tape and laminating which are used for many different sorts of applications as the selection follow.

BOPP film is a packing film with a superior mechanical nature, transparent and moisture proof formed through the processing of draft Bi-axially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) vertically and horizontally.
BOPP普通薄膜（双向拉伸聚丙烯）用于印刷和复合

BOPP薄膜广泛用于普通、复合、哑光和镀铝薄膜，适用于许多不同的应用选择，如纸箱、花卉、礼品、食品、糖果、复合、CD、DVD、VCD、香烟和包装盒、包装胶带等，根据其用途如印刷、服装、胶带和复合，用于许多不同的应用选择。

BOPP薄膜是一种具有优异机械性能、透明和防潮的包装薄膜，通过双向拉伸聚丙烯（BOPP）工艺制成。

Loading Details · 装载详情
1. Use 20GP, 40GP, 40HQ.
2. Per 20GP: With pallets, can load 12-15 tons. If the width of the roll is less than 500MM (such as 350MM), can load 12 tons, otherwise, width of the roll is more than 500MM (such as 900MM) can load about 15 tons. Without pallets, per 20GP can load 16-18 tons, of course the width of the roll is the key for the quantity loading under the same case.
3. Per 40GP: With pallets (usually use 40HQ), can load 24-25 tons. Without pallets, per 40GP can load 26-27 tons, of course the width of the roll is the key for the quantity loading under the same case.
