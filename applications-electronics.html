⚡ Capacitor Films

Ultra-thin, high-purity BOPP and BOPET films vacuum-metallized to form the dielectric and electrode layers used inside film capacitors. Manufactured to extremely tight thickness tolerances with reinforced edge margins for high electric field stress resistance.

  • Thickness Range: 3-12 μm for capacitor applications
  • Metallization: Zinc/aluminum alloy or pure aluminum
  • Reinforced Edge: 1.5-2.5mm edge margins for dielectric strength
  • Purity: Capacitor-grade high purity

🔌 Electrical Insulation

BOPET and BOPP films for electrical insulation in motors, transformers, and electronic devices. Excellent dielectric strength and thermal stability.

  • BOPET Clear Film: High dielectric strength for insulation
  • BOPP Anti-Static Film: Electronics protection
  • Thermal Stability: Operating range -60°C to 130°C
