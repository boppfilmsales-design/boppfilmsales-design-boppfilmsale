Matt BOPS Window Envelop Film

哑光BOPS窗口信封薄膜
Matt BOPS Window Envelop Film features a fine matte surface texture that provides a premium, low-glare finish. The matt surface offers excellent readability through the window while maintaining high stiffness and good printability. Ideal for elegant business envelopes and high-end direct mail applications.
哑光BOPS窗口信封薄膜具有精细的哑光表面纹理，提供高档、低反光效果。哑光表面通过窗口提供优异的易读性，同时保持高挺度和良好的印刷性能。适用于优雅的商务信封和高端直邮应用。
