BOPP Plain Film · BOPP普通薄膜
Thickness 3–75μm · Width 250–10,000mm

Bi-axially oriented polypropylene film manufactured via casting sheet extrusion and biaxial orientation. Excellent clarity, mechanical strength, and dimensional stability. Surface can be corona-treated on one or both sides for ink adhesion. Widely used in carton box overwrap, printing, lamination, food packaging, CD/DVD sleeves and general flexible packaging. 通过流延挤出和双向拉伸工艺生产的双向拉伸聚丙烯薄膜，具有优异的透明度、机械强度和尺寸稳定性。

BOPP Heat Sealable Film · BOPP热封薄膜
Seal Temp 100–140°C · One/Two-side

Co-extruded multilayer BOPP film with heat-seal layer engineered for low initiation temperature and strong, consistent seals on automated form-fill-seal and wrapping lines. Excellent hot-tack strength prevents seal failure during high-speed production. 共挤多层BOPP薄膜，热封层经特殊设计，具有较低的起封温度和优异的热封强度。

BOPP Pearlized Film · BOPP珠光薄膜
Density 0.55–0.75 g/cm³ · Opacity ≥75%

White opaque co-extruded BOPP film with micro-voided core layer creating distinctive pearlescent luster similar to coated paper, but with superior moisture resistance and lower density. Heat sealable on both sides with one-side corona treatment. Built-in antistatic properties. 白色不透明共挤BOPP薄膜，内含微孔发泡芯层，具有独特的珠光光泽。

BOPET Clear Film · BOPET透明薄膜
Thickness 4.5–350μm · Tensile ≥150MPa

High-clarity biaxially oriented polyester (PET) film offering outstanding mechanical strength, dimensional stability and chemical resistance far exceeding polyolefin films. Used as structural layer in multi-ply flexible packaging laminates and stand-alone film for technical applications. 高透明双向拉伸聚酯薄膜，具有远超聚烯烃薄膜的机械强度、尺寸稳定性和耐化学性。

POF Shrink Film · POF热缩薄膜
High Clarity · High Shrink Ratio · FDA Compliant

Polyolefin (POF) shrink film combining excellent optical clarity with high shrink ratio and strong, reliable seals across a wide sealing temperature window. PVDC Coating Film (K Film) · PVDC涂层薄膜
Exceptional Gas & Moisture Barrier

BOPP, BOPA or BOPET base film coated with PVDC resin, dramatically improving gas, moisture and aroma barrier performance. Widely known as "K film" or "KOP/KPET". The go-to solution for extending shelf life of moisture- and oxygen-sensitive products. BOPP、BOPA或BOPET基膜涂覆PVDC树脂，显著提升阻气、阻湿及保香性能。业内称为"K膜"。

BOPP Capacitor Film · BOPP电容器薄膜
Thickness 3–18μm · High Dielectric Strength

Ultra-thin, high-purity BOPP film vacuum-metallized with zinc/aluminum or pure aluminum to form dielectric and electrode layers inside film capacitors. Manufactured to extremely tight thickness tolerances with reinforced edge margins. Two types: clear capacitor film (M-PP, S-PP) and rough capacitor film (R-PP, RR-PP). 超薄高纯度BOPP薄膜，经真空镀铝处理，形成薄膜电容器内部的介质层及电极层。
