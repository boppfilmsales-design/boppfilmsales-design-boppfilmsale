📏 Full Customization Capability

We understand that every application has unique requirements. That's why we offer comprehensive customization across all our product categories — from film thickness and width to paper grammage and packaging configurations.

Our R&D team works directly with customers to develop tailored solutions that meet exact performance criteria. Whether you need a specific thickness for barrier properties, a particular width for your packaging machine, or a custom core size for your production line, we can manufacture to your precise specifications.

🎞️ Film Customization Parameters

  • Thickness Range: 3μm (capacitor film) to 350μm (BOPET), custom gauges available
  • Width Range: 250mm to 10,000mm, slit to any custom width
  • Core Size: 1", 2", 3", 6" diameter, or custom specifications
  • Roll Length: 200m to 12,000m depending on product and gauge
  • Surface Treatment: Corona treatment, coating, printing, lamination
  • Color & Appearance: Clear, tinted, pearlized, metallized, printed
  • Special Properties: Anti-static, anti-fog, heat sealable, high barrier

📄 Paper Customization Parameters

  • Grammage: 48-100 g/m² (LWC), 70/75/80 g/m² (copy paper)
  • Sizes: A4, letter, legal, A3, foolscap, 8.5"×11", 8.5"×13", custom sheet sizes
  • Brightness: Grade A (94-96%), Grade A+ (98-100%), Grade A++ (104-106%)
  • Jumbo Rolls: Up to 1500mm width, 2000kg roll weight
  • Core Size: 76mm (3") or 152.4mm (6")
  • Packaging: Custom ream count, carton design, pallet configuration
  • Branding: Custom ream wrappers and carton printing with customer logo

🔬 Sample & Development Process

We follow a structured process to ensure every custom product meets your expectations:

  • Step 1 — Consultation: Our technical team discusses your requirements in detail
  • Step 2 — Feasibility Assessment: We evaluate manufacturing feasibility and provide initial pricing
  • Step 3 — Sample Production: Produce trial samples for your evaluation and testing
  • Step 4 — Testing & Approval: You test the samples; we refine if needed
  • Step 5 — Bulk Production: Upon approval, we proceed with full production
  • Step 6 — Quality Assurance: Full quality control and inspection before shipment
