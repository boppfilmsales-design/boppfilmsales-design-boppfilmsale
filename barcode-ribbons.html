🖨️ Barcode Ribbons · 条码碳带

蜡基/树脂/蜡基树脂混合热转印碳带
Color Ribbon (Wax/resin) for barcode label printing. High opacity, vibrant colours. High resolution, good printing of small characters and fine lines. Good mechanical resistance against smearing and scratching. High sensitivity for increased print-head life. Very flexible, good results on all type of paper, cardboard and synthetic substrates and all printers equipped with near-edge print heads. Recommended label stock: Vellum, matt-and gloss-coated paper, cast-coated paper like Chromolux. Cardboard materials. PE, PP, PS, PET and other synthetic films.
彩色碳带（蜡基/树脂）用于条码标签印刷。高不透明度，鲜艳色彩。高分辨率，可打印细小字符和线条。良好的抗刮擦和耐磨性。高灵敏度，延长打印头寿命。非常灵活，适用于所有纸张、纸板和合成材料以及所有配备近边打印头的打印机。推荐标签材料：牛皮纸、哑光和光面铜版纸、铸涂纸如Chromolux。纸板材料。PE、PP、PS、PET等合成薄膜。
