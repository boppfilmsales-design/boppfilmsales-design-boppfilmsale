🌍 Global Market Coverage

With over 20 years of export experience, AEC GROUP has established a strong presence in markets across Asia, the Middle East, Africa, Europe, and the Americas. We understand the unique requirements of each market and have built long-term relationships with distributors, manufacturers, and end-users worldwide.

Our export portfolio spans flexible packaging films, paper products, adhesive materials, and industrial supplies. We serve diverse industries including food processing, pharmaceuticals, tobacco, printing, electronics, agriculture, and consumer goods.

📋 Documentation & Compliance

Our experienced export team handles all aspects of international trade documentation and regulatory compliance:

  • Letters of Credit (LC): Full LC documentation preparation and negotiation
  • Export Certificates: Certificate of Origin, Phytosanitary Certificates, Health Certificates, Free Sale Certificates
  • Customs Documentation: Commercial invoices, packing lists, bills of lading, air waybills
  • Regulatory Compliance: FDA, EU REACH, RoHS, food safety standards, and country-specific regulations
  • Insurance: Cargo insurance arrangements for all shipping methods
  • Inspection: Pre-shipment inspection coordination (SGS, Bureau Veritas, etc.)

🚢 Logistics & Shipping

We manage all aspects of international logistics to ensure timely and cost-effective delivery:

  • Sea Freight: FCL and LCL shipments from Shanghai, Ningbo, and Qingdao ports
  • Air Freight: Express air cargo for urgent orders
  • Rail Freight: China-Europe rail service for cost-effective land transport
  • Multimodal: Combined transport solutions for optimal cost and speed
  • Warehousing: Bonded warehouse options for inventory management
  • Door-to-Door: Full logistics management from factory to your warehouse

💼 Trade Finance

We offer flexible payment terms and trade finance solutions to support your business:

  • Letters of Credit (L/C) at sight and usance
  • Telegraphic Transfer (T/T) with deposit and balance
  • Documents against Payment (D/P)
  • Open Account for established partners
  • Export credit insurance for risk mitigation
