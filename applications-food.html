🎞️ Film Solutions for Food Packaging

Our flexible packaging films serve every segment of the food industry — from snack wrappers to frozen food bags, from fresh produce packaging to retort pouches. We offer BOPP, BOPET, CPP, and POF films in custom specifications to match your exact packaging requirements.

🍿 Snack & Confectionery

BOPP matte and glossy films for candy wrappers, chip bags, and snack packaging. Excellent clarity, printability, and moisture barrier properties keep products fresh and visually appealing.

  • BOPP Matte Film: Premium soft-touch finish for high-end confectionery
  • BOPP Glossy Film: High clarity for vibrant printed snack packaging
  • BOPP Heat Sealable: Reliable sealing for high-speed packaging lines
  • Metallized BOPP: Enhanced barrier for moisture and light-sensitive snacks

🥬 Fresh Food & Produce

Films designed to extend shelf life and maintain freshness of fruits, vegetables, and fresh food products. Excellent breathability control and moisture barrier properties.

  • BOPP Anti-Fog Film: Prevents condensation in fresh food packaging
  • CPP Film: Excellent clarity and heat sealing for food containers
  • POF Shrink Film: Tight wrapping for fresh produce bundling

🧊 Frozen & Retort Food

High-performance films that withstand extreme temperatures — from frozen storage to high-temperature retour sterilization.

  • BOPA Film: Outstanding puncture resistance for frozen food
  • BOPET Film: Dimensional stability at extreme temperatures
  • Retort CPP: Withstands 121°C sterilization for shelf-stable food
  • VMCPP: Vacuum metallized CPP for extended shelf life
