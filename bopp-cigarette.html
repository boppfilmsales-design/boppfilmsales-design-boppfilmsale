Product Description
BOPP tobacco film or BOPP cigarette film also called as BOPP shrinkage cigarette film, Polypropylene cigar film, BOPP soft cigarette pack film, BOPP hard rigid tobacco film, BOPP tobacco pack film, BOPP tear tape tobacco film. Well-fitting to machine, suitable for high speed packing of all kinds of soft pack. Special hot slip ability on surface, can avoid machine-blocking, sticking etc. Quick heat sealing at low temperature and pressure. Good foldability after forming.
