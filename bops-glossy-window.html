Glossy BOPS Window Envelop Film

光亮BOPS窗口信封薄膜
Glossy BOPS Window Envelop Film is a biaxially oriented polystyrene film with high clarity and gloss, specifically designed for window envelope applications. The glossy surface provides excellent transparency for address visibility while maintaining high stiffness and good printability.
光亮BOPS窗口信封薄膜是一种双向拉伸聚苯乙烯薄膜，具有高透明度和光泽，专为窗口信封应用设计。光亮表面提供优异的地址可见性透明度，同时保持高挺度和良好的印刷性能。
