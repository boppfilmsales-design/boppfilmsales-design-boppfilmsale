BOPP Heat Sealable Film

Two Sides Heat Sealable Polypropylene Film

双面热封聚丙烯薄膜 · 双面热封BOPP薄膜
Heat Sealable BOPP Film. Heat sealable BOPP film production line consists of casting sheet extrusion, biaxial orientation, after treatment, rolling, cutting, and automatic control. It can produce BOPP films of various specifications and stable dimensions, with good mechanical and electrical performances, good heat resistance, cold resistance and gastightness.
热封BOPP薄膜。热封BOPP薄膜生产线由流延挤出、双向拉伸、后处理、卷绕、切割和自动控制组成。可生产各种规格和稳定尺寸的BOPP薄膜，具有良好的机械和电气性能，良好的耐热性、耐寒性和气密性。
