Bopet Plain Film

普通膜
Plain BOPET (Biaxially Oriented Polyester) film for general packaging and industrial applications. Offers excellent clarity, mechanical strength and dimensional stability. Non-metallized, transparent grade suitable for printing, lamination and protective wrapping.
普通BOPET（双向拉伸聚酯）薄膜，适用于通用包装及工业应用。具有优异的透明度、机械强度和尺寸稳定性。非镀铝透明级，适用于印刷、复合及保护性包装。
