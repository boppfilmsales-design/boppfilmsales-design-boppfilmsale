PVDC coating film -K film (BOPP/BOPA/BOPET film coated PVDC)

1 Types for PVDC coating film -K film
PVDC coating film -K film (BOPP/BOPA/BOPET film coated PVDC)
KOPP (BOPP film, coated on both sides heat sealing barrier PVDC coating)
KPA (BOPA film, coated on both sides heat sealing barrier PVDC coating)
KPET (BOPET film, coated on both sides heat sealing barrier PVDC coating)
KPE (PE film, coated on both sides heat sealing barrier PVDC coating)
Thickness of PVDC coating film: 12UM (for KPET), 21UM (for KOPP), 17UM (for KPA)...
Width of PVDC coating film: 120-1500MM Length: 200-4000M Inner core:1" 2" 3" 6".

2 Features of PVDC coating film -K film (BOPP/BOPA/BOPET film coated PVDC)
a. excellent gas barrier properties, excellent in fragrance preservation property
b. excellent low-temperature heat-sealing properties, sealing and smoothing seed
very good transparent performance

3 main purposes for PVDC coating film -K film (BOPP/BOPA/BOPET film coated PVDC):
a. cigarette packaging b snack packaging c. dessert packaging
PVDC涂层薄膜-K膜（BOPP/BOPA/BOPET薄膜涂覆PVDC）

1. PVDC涂层薄膜-K膜的类型
PVDC涂层薄膜-K膜（BOPP/BOPA/BOPET薄膜涂覆PVDC）
KOPP（BOPP薄膜，双面热封阻隔PVDC涂层）
KPA（BOPA薄膜，双面热封阻隔PVDC涂层）
KPET（BOPET薄膜，双面热封阻隔PVDC涂层）
KPE（PE薄膜，双面热封阻隔PVDC涂层）
PVDC涂层薄膜厚度：12微米（KPET）、21微米（KOPP）、17微米（KPA）...
PVDC涂层薄膜宽度：120-1500毫米，长度：200-4000米，纸芯：1英寸、2英寸、3英寸、6英寸。

2. PVDC涂层薄膜-K膜的特性（BOPP/BOPA/BOPET薄膜涂覆PVDC）
a. 优异的阻隔性能，优异的保香性能
b. 优异的低温热封性能，封合平滑度
良好的透明性能

3. PVDC涂层薄膜-K膜的主要用途（BOPP/BOPA/BOPET薄膜涂覆PVDC）：
a. 烟草包装 b. 休闲食品包装 c. 甜点包装
