Polyester Film for Transfer Printing

转印用聚酯薄膜 / BOPET TTR基膜
BOPET TTR film (polyester film for transfer print) were basic film for BOPET Thermal Transfer color Ribbons film, HOT STAMPING FILM. Thickness: 4.4, 4.5, 4.55, 4.6, 4.8, 5.0, 5.7, 6.0μm. Width: 500, 600, 910, 980, 1010, 1200, 1500mm. Length: 15000, 17000, 20000, 24000, 37000m. Feature: clear/transparent. Thermal Transfer Ribbons have stability features in shrinkage and thickness stability, good heat resistance, easy processing in thermal transfer. Suitable for use in bar code printers, fax machines and transfer ribbon.
BOPET TTR薄膜（转印用聚酯薄膜）是BOPET热转印彩色碳带薄膜、烫金膜的基础薄膜。厚度：4.4、4.5、4.55、4.6、4.8、5.0、5.7、6.0微米。宽度：500、600、910、980、1010、1200、1500mm。长度：15000、17000、20000、24000、37000米。特性：透明/透明。热转印碳带在收缩率和厚度稳定性方面具有稳定性，耐热性好，热转印加工容易。适用于条码打印机、传真机和转印碳带。
