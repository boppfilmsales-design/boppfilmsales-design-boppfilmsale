Bopp cigarette box wrapping film

BOPP香烟盒包装膜
BOPP cigarette tobacco film generally used for soft/hard tobacco pack and used for modern high/slow packing machines. Transparent biaxially oriented polypropylene cigarette film featured coextruded heat sealable on both sides, incorporated with anti-static protection. Thickness: 18,19,20,21,22,25 Microns. Width: 90,110,118,120,135,150,220,350MM. Length: 2000M, 2800M, 3000M, 3500M. Well-fitting to machine, suitable for high speed packing of all kinds of soft pack. Special hot slip ability on surface, can avoid machine-blocking, sticking etc. Quick heat sealing at low temperature and pressure. Good foldability after forming. Customer's own requirement is available for us such as printed OEM logos and private label or particular size and package style.
