✂️ Film Slitting Machine · 薄膜分切机

高精度分切机，适用于BOPP、BOPET、CPP薄膜
Model of SDF-F 1300 Plastic Extrusion Compound Uni. High-precision slitting machine for BOPP, BOPET, CPP films. Width up to 10,000mm, thickness 3-350μm. Automatic tension control, digital display, high-speed operation. PLC programmable controller with 6" man-machine interface. AC motor drive with vector frequency conversion.
SDF-F 1300型塑料挤出复合一体机。高精度分切机，适用于BOPP、BOPET、CPP薄膜。幅宽可达10,000mm，厚度3-350微米。自动张力控制，数字显示，高速运行。PLC可编程控制器，6人机界面操作。交流电机驱动，矢量变频控制。
